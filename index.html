About

Potomac Media Group is a small full-service independently run social media agency based out of the Washington, DC metro area. We create complete marketing campaigns for organizations aimed at increasing awareness and action towards a social issue or cause to ensure that your organization's message gets delivered to the right people who can help influence real change. We do everything from the marketing strategy and promotion of your cause or issue on social media to creating content and interacting with followers so you get the results that your organization is looking for while saving your organization time and resources.
